community GUIDELINES

We want everyone to enjoy using Hapity and the following guidelines are intended to keep Hapity an open, safe and friendly community. Do not use Hapity for any unlawful purposes or in furtherance of any illegal activities Do not post content that is pornographic or overtly sexual

Do not post content that is explicitly graphic: this includes, but is not limited to animal abuse or bodily harm

Do not post content that contains or may incite violent, illegal, sexist, racist, blasphemous, homophobic or dangerous activities

Do not impersonate to mislead, confuse or deceive others: parody, commentary or fan accounts should be clearly labelled to avoid any such confusion

Do not spam: user abuse and technical abuse are not tolerated, and may result in permanent account suspension. Examples of abuse and spam include but are not limited to:

  • - Malware / Phishing: publishing or linking to malicious content intended to damage or disrupt another user’s account or device, or to compromise a user’s privacy or log-in information
  • - Serial Accounts: creating serial accounts for disruptive or abusive purposes
  • - Selling Accounts: purchasing or selling Hapity accounts
  • - Invitation Spam: repeatedly following and unfollowing people
  • - Broadcast Spam:
    • Broadcasting for the sole purpose of directing users to an external site or service
    • Posting deliberately misleading broadcast titles, especially with the intent to redirect the viewer to an external site or service
    • Paying to have a broadcast ‘shared’ to increase the popularity of the content
    • Using serial accounts to auto ‘share’ broadcasts to increase popularity
    • Posting large numbers of unsolicited comments, especially in an attempt to advertise a service or link
    • Using or promoting third-party sites or services that claim to get you more followers

Be nice to each other and respect others’ privacy: do not abuse or harass others, or post others’ private information. (Remember that although you may consider certain information to be private, not all postings of such information may be a violation of this policy. We may consider the context and nature of the information posted, local privacy laws, and other case-specific facts when determining if this policy has been violated.) Examples of private information include but are not limited to:

  • - National identity numbers
  • - Credit card information
  • - Addresses or locations that are considered and treated private
  • - Non-public, personal phone numbers and emails
  • - Intimate videos or photos taken or distributed without the subject's consent
  • - Videos or images that are considered and treated as private under applicable laws

We reserve the right to allow sensitive content when we consider it to be artistic, educational, scientific or newsworthy
